Our Story

It Started With One Open Door

In 2008, our founder opened the doors of a single-room home to five orphaned children with no other place to go. There was no grand plan — only a conviction that every child, regardless of circumstance, deserved safety, love, and a future.

Word spread. More children came. A small community of volunteers, neighbours, and early donors rallied around the vision. Within three years, Rain Edge International had grown into a fully registered orphanage home serving over 60 children and had broken ground on its first primary school.

Today, we operate a thriving ecosystem — a residential home, a primary school, a secondary school, and a portfolio of social enterprises including a water packaging plant and a bakery — all working together to make the organisation self-sustaining and to give every child in our care a genuine shot at a prosperous future.

Purpose

What We Stand For

Every decision we make — from how we run our home to how we invest enterprise income — is anchored to these two foundational statements.

🎯
Mission

Restoring dignity, unlocking potential.

To provide every orphaned and vulnerable child with a secure home, quality education from primary through secondary school, and access to pathways of economic empowerment — all sustained by ethical, community-rooted social enterprise.

🌅
Vision

A world where no child's future is determined by their past.

We envision communities where every child — regardless of background, loss, or circumstance — has the love, education, and economic foundation needed to live a dignified, purposeful, and self-sustaining life.

Our DNA

Core Values

These six principles guide every interaction, every program, and every business decision we make at Rain Edge International.

❤️

Unconditional Love

Every child in our care receives love that does not depend on behaviour, background, or academic performance. Love is our first programme.

🎓

Education as a Right

We believe quality education is a fundamental right — not a privilege — and we work tirelessly to ensure every child accesses it free of charge.

🌱

Sustainability

Our enterprise model ensures we are not dependent on donations alone. We build businesses that generate income, create jobs, and fund our mission in perpetuity.

🤝

Community Partnership

We are embedded in the communities we serve. We hire locally, source locally, and ensure the benefits of our enterprises flow back into the surrounding neighbourhood.

🔍

Transparency & Accountability

We publish our annual reports, financial summaries, and impact data. Donors, partners, and the public deserve to know exactly how every shilling is used.

✊

Child Dignity First

We never use children for fundraising imagery without consent and context. Every communication upholds the dignity and privacy of the children we serve.

Our Beneficiaries

Who We Serve

Rain Edge International serves three interconnected groups — each part of a broader ecosystem of care and development.

🏠

Orphaned Children

The core of our mission. We provide residential care to children who have lost one or both parents and have no family support system.

  • Safe residential accommodation
  • Three nutritious meals daily
  • Medical & psychological care
  • Clothing, personal items & recreation
  • Full school sponsorship from primary to secondary
📚

Day Students

Our schools are open to day scholars from the surrounding community, many of whom come from low-income households. Education fees are subsidised.

  • Quality primary education
  • Structured secondary school curriculum
  • School feeding programme
  • Academic scholarships for top performers
  • Career guidance & mentorship
🌍

The Wider Community

Our enterprises create tangible benefits for the communities around us — not just economic, but social and public health outcomes too.

  • Affordable packaged drinking water
  • Fresh bread and baked goods daily
  • Local employment (20+ jobs created)
  • Vocational training for youth
  • Agricultural produce for local markets
Our Journey

15 Years of Milestones

From a single room to a full ecosystem — the milestones that have shaped who we are today.

2008
2008 — Founded

The First Home Opens

Founder opens a single residential room to five orphaned children. Rain Edge International is officially registered as a non-profit organisation.

2010 — Education

Primary School Breaks Ground

With support from early donors and volunteers, we begin construction of our first primary school classrooms, opening enrolment to both resident and day students.

2010
2013
2013 — Expansion

New Residential Block & 100 Children

A new dormitory block is completed, allowing the home to expand its capacity to 100 children. A full-time medical nurse joins the team.

2015 — Enterprise

Water Packaging Plant Launched

Our first social enterprise — a pure water packaging plant — begins operations, selling to local markets and institutions. Revenue directly funds the orphanage's operational costs.

2015
2017
2017 — Education

Secondary School Opens

Rain Edge Secondary School admits its first cohort of students, providing a seamless educational pathway from primary through to national examinations for all our children.

2019 — Enterprise

Bakery & Farm Established

We diversify our enterprise portfolio with a community bakery and a productive farm — reducing food costs for the home and creating 15 full-time jobs for community members.

2019
2023
2023 — Achievement

First University Graduates

Our pioneering cohort of children — those who entered the home in the early years — graduate from university with degrees in medicine, engineering, and education. A defining milestone.

Today — Growing

Expanding Ventures & Deeper Impact

With 500+ children served, two accredited schools, three social enterprises, and new manufacturing ventures in development — Rain Edge International continues to grow and deepen its community impact.
