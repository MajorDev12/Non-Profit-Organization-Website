Who We Are

A Home That Grows Into a Future

"Every child deserves more than survival — they deserve to dream, learn, and lead."

— Founder, Rain Edge International

Rain Edge International started as an orphanage home. Today it is a fully-integrated ecosystem — providing shelter, love, and top-quality education from primary through secondary school, all sustained by our growing portfolio of social enterprises.

Our Enterprises

Self-Sustaining Industries

View All Enterprises

Our social enterprises generate income, create local jobs, and directly fund the care and education of our children.

💧

Pure Water Packaging

Clean, affordable drinking water for local communities. Our water plant ensures safe drinking water access while generating sustainable revenue.

🍞

Bakery

Fresh baked goods produced daily — serving schools and markets, while providing vocational training for older youth in our programs.

🌿

Agriculture & Farming

A productive farm supplying food for the orphanage and schools, reducing costs and teaching children sustainable agricultural practices.

⚡

Expanding Ventures

New ventures in manufacturing and agro-processing are in development — deepening our self-sustainability and community impact.
